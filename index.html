Our Mission

We created this website to help solve a world problem. Over the last year things have been very rough, especially with covid. The goal of this application is to help the user locate a nearby soup kitchen, a shelter, or both if needed. One of the most important things in life is sticking together as a community. As the century's old saying goes, 'A chain is only as strong as it's weakest link'. Helping others in need is the best way to persevere through these difficult times and mend this divided country.
